About 2-min Invoice Gen

2-min Invoice Gen is a simple, fast, and professional invoice generator designed to help freelancers, small businesses, and entrepreneurs create beautiful invoices in minutes.

Our Mission

We believe that creating professional invoices shouldn't be complicated or time-consuming. Our mission is to provide a tool that makes invoicing quick, easy, and effective.

Features

  • Create professional invoices in under 2 minutes
  • Download as PDF with one click
  • Multiple currency support
  • Beautiful, customizable templates
  • No registration required for free version
  • Premium features for power users

Team

2-min Invoice Gen is developed by a small team of developers and designers who understand the needs of freelancers and small business owners.

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Is 2-min Invoice Gen free to use?

Yes! Our basic invoice generation features are completely free to use. We offer premium features for users who need more advanced functionality.

Do I need to create an account?

No, you can use the basic features without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your invoices and access them later.

What currencies are supported?

We support multiple currencies including USD, INR, EUR, GBP, AUD, and more. You can select your preferred currency from the dropdown menu.

Premium Features

What do I get with the premium version?

Premium features include watermark-free PDFs, unlimited templates, custom branding, invoice history, and priority support.

How much does premium cost?

Premium subscription costs ₹75 for 3 months of access to all premium features.

Can I cancel my premium subscription?

Yes, you can cancel at any time. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Technical Questions

What browsers are supported?

2-min Invoice Gen works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Is my data secure?

We take data security seriously. Your invoice data is processed securely and we don't store sensitive financial information.
